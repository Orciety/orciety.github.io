I'm a

Composer, music producer, pianist, sound designer

Hey there!

I'm Thomas, a music enthusiast from Canada with a huge interest in piano and composition. I've been studying piano performance and musical composition for the past 8+ years, and have a deep understanding of music theory. Since 2022, I have also taken an interest in music production and sound design.

Throughout my musical journey, I have been lucky enough to achieve recognition for my work. I was nominated as one of the winners of the Oscar Bookbinder Composition Competition in 2024. The previous year, I received the Royal Conservatory of Music (RCM) Piano Level 8 National Gold Medal and was nominated as a winner of the Louise MacPherson Memorial Composition National Award.

I also reached the semi-finals at the RCM Music Lights the Way competition. In 2022, I was additionally named the London Young Musician's Composer/Pianist of the Year. Finally, I was chosen as an Artist of the Year Gold Absolute Winner from the International Youth Music Competition in 2021.
