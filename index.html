I'm

Composer, music producer, pianist, sound designer

Hey there!

I'm Thomas, a music enthusiast living in Canada with a deep passion for piano and composition. I have been studing piano performance and musical composition during the past 8+ years, and I have expanded my interest into music production and sound design since 2022.

Throughout my musical journey, I have been fortunate to receive recognition for my efforts. I received the 2023 Royal Conservatory of Music Piano Level 8 National Gold Medal and was named the London Young Musician's Composer/Pianist of the Year in 2022. In 2024, I won the Oscar Bookbinder Composition Competition at the Kiwanis Music Festival of Guelph. Additionally, I was the Artists of the Year Gold Absolute Winner from the International Youth Music Competition in 2021, and I became a semi-finalist at the RCM Music Lights the Way competition in 2023.

My passion for music drives me to continually refine my skills. Whether you need a pianist, composer, or help with music production and sound design, I can bring your vision to life. Let's collaborate and create something extraordinary together!
