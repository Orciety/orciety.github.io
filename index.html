Hey There!

Hey there, I'm a music enthusiast from Canada.

I had the honor of showcasing my compositions at the Regina Contemporary Showcase 2022 and 2023, and I'm grateful for the opportunities and recognition my compositions have received. I am proud to have won several awards for my compositions, including five scholarships and first place five times, as well as second place twice, at the Saskatchewan Registered Music Teachers' Association (SRMTA) Student Composer Competitions. I also received a scholarship and first place in the national Canadian Federal Music Teachers' Association (CFMTA) Student Composer Competition in 2022, with a third place in 2023 and an honorable mention in 2021. In addition, my compositions have won several medals and trophies at composition competitions in Canada, the USA, and the UK. I've also received twelve scholarships at Regina Music Festivals and Saskatchewan Music Festivals and reached the semi-finals at RCM Music Lights the Way piano competition.

To date, I have composed more than 30 pieces and am currently working on a set of 24 preludes inspired by Chopin and Bach, as well as exploring cinematic music. My big dream is to publish an album of my compositions someday! I'll keep this website updated with my progress and achievements, so stay tuned!
